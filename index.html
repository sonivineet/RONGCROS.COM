Nestled near the Grand Palace, RONGROS is more than a restaurant. It's a celebration of Thai food philosophy: the balance of sweet, salty, sour, and spicy. Each dish is a conversation between heritage and contemporary technique.

We cook for Bangkok locals who know quality. We welcome travelers who seek authenticity. Our kitchen honors tradition while refusing to be bound by it. Every ingredient is chosen with intention. Every plate reflects our commitment to flavor over filler.

Everyone is welcome here. We are proudly LGBTQ+ friendly, celebrating diversity as much as we celebrate Thai cuisine.
